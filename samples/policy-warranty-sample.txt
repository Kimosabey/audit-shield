Policy Bulletin AW-2024 (Maintenance & Warranty) — Sample for AuditShield ingest
==================================================================================

Section 3 — Parts warranty
3.1 Standard warranty for catalog part WDG-4401 (packaged RTU door gasket kit)
is twenty-four (24) months from date of installation or thirty (30) months from
date of shipment, whichever occurs first. Labor for replacement is covered only
when performed by an authorized service partner under work order class "W-RTU".

3.2 Corrective maintenance is defined as repairs performed after equipment failure
or alarm that restores the unit to nominal operation; it excludes scheduled
preventive tasks listed in SOP-HVAC-114.

Section 4 — Lockout / Tagout (LOTO)
4.1 For packaged RTU units, LOTO shall follow OSHA 1910.147 and site bulletin
MB-2024-07. Minimum steps: (a) notify operations, (b) isolate all energy sources,
(c) apply locks and tags per plant map, (d) verify zero energy before work,
(e) brief relief before shift change using form LOTO-RTU-01.

Section 5 — Pressure / safety (PRV-03)
5.1 Chilled-water riser PRV-03 maximum allowable working pressure (MAWP) is
175 psig. Relief valve RV-CHW-03 shall be set to open at 185 psig and proof-tested
per SE-44 annually.

Section 6 — Record retention
6.1 Signed commissioning checklists for air-handling units (AHUs) must be retained
for not less than seven (7) years from the date the system is accepted, per the
audit program ACX-9.

Cross-reference note (contradiction check sample)
Bulletin MB-2024-07 states filter media inspection intervals shall not exceed 90
days for high-dust sites. SOP-HVAC-114 sets a default 180-day filter change for
standard sites; sites classified "high-dust" in the facility registry must follow
the stricter 90-day interval in MB-2024-07 (no conflict when classification applies).
